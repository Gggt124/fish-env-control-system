<objective>
Close the UAT test 1 major gap: the boot splash (and therefore the dashboard once 18-03 is in) renders mirrored on the X axis, with a cyan/light-blue background instead of true black, and a bright white vertical band on the right portion of the screen. The two cheapest source-only fixes are (1) flipping the `esp_lcd_panel_mirror` flags from `(false, true)` to `(true, false)` to correct horizontal orientation, and (2) adding an explicit `esp_lcd_panel_set_gap(s_panel_handle, 0, 0)` to force the panel coordinate origin. If those two fixes are not enough (the white band is the signature of an ST7789 panel being driven by the ILI9341 init sequence), the plan falls back to adding the `espressif/esp_lcd_st7789` driver dependency gated behind a build-time `#ifdef APP_TEMPLATE_TFT_PANEL_ST7789` so the user can flip a single commented-out `#define` in `components/app_config/app_config.h` and rebuild — no code edits required. A human-verify checkpoint sits between the cheap fixes and the driver swap so the fallback is only invoked if the cheap fixes fail.
</objective>
