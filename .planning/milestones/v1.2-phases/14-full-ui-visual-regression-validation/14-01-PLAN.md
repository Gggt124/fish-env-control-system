<threat_model>
### T-14-01: Screenshot evidence overstates runtime coverage
- Severity: low
- Surface: evidence reports and milestone closeout
- Risk: simulated browser screenshots could be mistaken for ESP32 hardware behavior.
- Mitigation: require an evidence-source label for every row and screenshot.
- Verification: inspect `evidence/README.md` and `browser-checks.md` for `device-backed`, `simulated`, `source-inspected`, or `not-run`.

### T-14-02: Captured screenshots expose local private data
- Severity: low
- Surface: committed screenshot artifacts
- Risk: screenshots may leak real SSIDs, MAC addresses, local IPs, or session details.
- Mitigation: privacy-review screenshots before commit and redact or omit sensitive evidence.
- Verification: evidence inventory includes a privacy-review result for every committed image.
</threat_model>
