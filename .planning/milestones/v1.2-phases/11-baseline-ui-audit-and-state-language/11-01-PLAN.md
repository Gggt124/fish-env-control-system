<threat_model>
### T-11-01: Evidence overstates runtime validation
- Severity: low
- Surface: audit report and screenshot inventory
- Risk: source inspection or simulated screenshots could be presented as device-backed behavior, hiding hardware-only failures.
- Mitigation: require an evidence-source label and limitation note for every page and interaction check.
- Verification: inspect `evidence/README.md` and the audit matrix for device-backed, simulated, source-inspected, or not-run labels.

### T-11-02: Captured evidence exposes local secrets
- Severity: low
- Surface: committed screenshots and evidence notes
- Risk: screenshots may expose credentials, session cookies, private SSIDs, MAC addresses, or sensitive local network details.
- Mitigation: capture only sanitized screens, redact sensitive identifiers before commit, and document the privacy review.
- Verification: inspect every committed screenshot and confirm the evidence inventory includes a privacy-review result.
</threat_model>
